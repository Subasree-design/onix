4 roles
Clinical
Consultant Radiologist
Remote (US)

Read studies on ONIX AI's browser-based viewer and help shape how the AI copilot supports reporting. Provide direct clinical feedback to the product team and be part of defining what responsible AI-assisted radiology looks like.

Responsibilities
  • Read and report studies across modalities using the ONIX AI viewer
  • Provide structured feedback on AI draft findings and report quality
  • Collaborate with the product team on clinical workflow improvements
  • Participate in case review sessions and quality assurance processes
Requirements
  • Board-certified radiologist with active US license
  • Experience across multiple modalities (CT, MRI, X-ray)
  • Comfortable working in a browser-based reading environment
  • Interest in AI-assisted radiology and clinical feedback loops
Engineering
Senior Frontend Engineer
Remote (Global)

Build the DICOM viewer, reporting surfaces, and collaboration tools radiologists use every day. You'll be working at the intersection of medical imaging, real-time collaboration, and AI — shaping the interface layer of a clinical platform.

Responsibilities
  • Build and maintain the DICOM / NIfTI viewer and annotation tooling
  • Develop the report editor, voice transcription UI, and case collaboration surfaces
  • Optimize rendering performance for large medical imaging datasets
  • Work closely with radiologists and clinical staff on usability feedback
Requirements
  • 5+ years of frontend engineering experience
  • Strong React and TypeScript skills; WebGL or Canvas experience a plus
  • Experience with medical imaging standards (DICOM, HL7) preferred
  • Passion for building tools used in high-stakes environments
Engineering
AI / ML Engineer
Remote (Global)

Develop and deploy models for image analysis, structured report drafting, and study prioritization. Work with large medical imaging datasets and contribute to the core AI layer that radiologists interact with every day.

Responsibilities
  • Train and fine-tune models for radiology image analysis tasks
  • Build structured report drafting pipelines using LLMs and clinical context
  • Design study prioritization and intelligent routing logic
  • Collaborate with radiologists to validate model outputs clinically
Requirements
  • 3+ years in applied ML / deep learning engineering
  • Experience with medical imaging datasets (DICOM, NIfTI)
  • Proficiency in Python, PyTorch or TensorFlow
  • Familiarity with LLM fine-tuning and clinical NLP a strong plus
Operations
Customer Success Manager
Walnut Creek, CA (hybrid)

Help hospitals, imaging centers, and teleradiology groups onboard and get the most out of ONIX AI. You'll be the bridge between our clinical platform and the teams using it daily — ensuring smooth implementation and long-term adoption.

Responsibilities
  • Manage onboarding for new hospital and imaging center partners
  • Build and maintain strong relationships with clinical and admin stakeholders
  • Track usage, identify friction points, and coordinate with the product team
  • Create training materials and run onboarding sessions for radiologist teams
Requirements
  • 3+ years in customer success, implementation, or account management
  • Experience in healthcare technology or SaaS preferred
  • Strong communicator with ability to engage clinical and technical stakeholders
  • Based in or willing to commute to Walnut Creek, CA (hybrid)
